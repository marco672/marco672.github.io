About Me

Hi! My name is Marco Belmonte, and I am a dynamic professional with a strong background in Law and a passion for embracing new challenges. Born in São Paulo, I graduated with a degree in Law in 2005 and have practiced as a lawyer for over 16 years. In 2007, I completed a postgraduate degree in Public Law, which enabled me to work as a professor in higher education. Later, I pursued a second specialization in Business Law.

Throughout my career, I have balanced my professional life with hobbies such as photography, theater, writing, and music. I have written legal articles for the "Diário de Comércio e Indústria", participated in creative courses, and taken part in several theatrical productions. I have lived in São Paulo, Sydney, and currently reside in Porto, experiences that have broadened my worldview and deepened my connection with different cultures. I am passionate about traveling and have explored countries like Argentina, Australia, Brazil, Mexico (Cancún), Chile, Hawaii, Jamaica, Portugal, Sweden, the USA, and more.

These travels have provided invaluable experiences, allowing me to immerse myself in new cultures and perspectives. Over time, I developed a passion for photography, particularly in capturing stunning landscapes from diverse locations. In 2022, I moved to Portugal, and by 2024, I decided to transition into the Information Technology field, seeking new growth and creative opportunities. At the end of that year, I began studying UX/UI Design, developing new skills in this area.

This journey inspired me to create this website, where I can share some of my photography and creative experiences. I hope you enjoy exploring it! If you are interested in collaborations or projects, feel free to get in touch. I would be delighted to connect with you!
