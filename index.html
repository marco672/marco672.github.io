About Me


Hi! My name is Marco Belmonte, a dynamic professional with a solid background in Law and a passion for new challenges. Born in São Paulo, I graduated in Law in 2005 and practiced as a lawyer for over 18 years. In 2007, I completed a postgraduate degree in Public Law, which allowed me to also work as a professor in higher education. Later, I pursued a second specialization in Business Law.

Throughout my career, I have always balanced my professional path with hobbies such as photography, theater and writing. I have written legal articles for the "Diário de Comércio e Indústria", participated in creative courses and some theatrical productions. I have lived in São Paulo, Sydney, and currently reside in Porto, which has broadened my worldview and deepened my connection with different cultures. I am passionate about traveling and have had the opportunity to explore places such as Argentina, Australia, Brazil, Cancún, Chile, Hawaii, Jamaica, Portugal, Sweden, USA, and others.

These travels have given me invaluable experiences, allowing me to immerse myself in new cultures and perspectives. Over time, I developed a passion for photography, especially capturing beautiful landscapes in diverse locations. In 2022, I moved to Portugal, and in 2024, I decided to transition into the Information Technology field, seeking growth and new creative opportunities. By the end of that year, I began studying UX/UI Design, focusing on usability, web development, prototyping, HTML, CSS, JavaScript and project management, developing new skills in this area.

This journey inspired me to create this website, where I can share some of my photography and my creative experiences. I hope you enjoy exploring it! If you are interested in collaborations or projects, feel free to get in touch. It would be a pleasure to connect with you!
