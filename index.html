About Me


Hi! I am Marco Belmonte, a dynamic professional with a strong background in Law and a love for new challenges. I graduated in Law in 2005 and practiced for over 18 years. In 2007, I completed a postgraduate degree in Public Law, which allowed me to teach in higher education, and later specialized in Business Law.


I have always balanced my career with hobbies like photography, theater, writing, and music. I have lived in São Paulo, Sydney, and Porto, and traveled to places like Argentina, Australia, Cancún, Chile, Hawaii, Jamaica, Portugal, Sweden, and the USA. These experiences have broadened my worldview and deepened my appreciation for different cultures.


In 2022, I moved to Portugal and, in 2024, transitioned into Information Technology, studying UX/UI Design to develop new skills. This website is a space to share my photography and creative experiences during my life. If you are interested in collaborations or projects, feel free to get in touch! It is pleasure to meet you!
